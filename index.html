About Me

Who Am I?

My name is Mehran Bakhshande student of Industrial Safety from Shahid Beheshti Medical University and I ranked 1648 among 144000 Math students. My fundamental reasons to choose Industrial Safety were progression, welfare and safety which arise of Industrial Science and its majors at the top of my priority. Industrial Safety is mixture of Industrial Engineering with engineering-based courses and Occupational Health with courses related to jobs’ environment
All the courses mentioned in Education section had been taught by practical and theorical science. This program is closer to some programs for instance HSE Engineering, Healthcare Systems Engineering or Occupational Health. Not only my inherent desire, but also similarity between your courses and mine encourage me to getting contact with you and asking for education under your supervision.

Consequently, my student life in university consists of three cardinal sections: Education, Voluntary works, Athletics.

Education

Manufacturing and repairing of tools and control on mankind machines have significant role in revaluation of today’s hectic life.
